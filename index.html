The Secret Next Door

A Hybrid Exhibition Experience for the Pinacoteca Nazionale di Bologna

"These paintings were taken from the city — and the city forgot. Bring them home."

Play the Story Read the Design Brief
Group GGU — Global Girlies United

Five paintings. Five lost churches. One secret.

Almost all the paintings in the Pinacoteca did not start there. About 200 years ago, under Napoleon, the churches of Bologna were closed, and their paintings were carried into one building. Our project helps people find these works again and bring them "home" on a city map.
